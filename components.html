Project Vision

Canvas gives every Amplifier user a visual command center. Instead of living entirely in the terminal, users get a sidebar that tracks all their sessions across projects.

Goal

A desktop companion that makes the terminal more productive, not less terminal.

Principles
  • Visibility, not action
  • Complement the CLI, never replace it
  • One app, one window
